Strength, Honor, Discipline

Mind and Body

Pure Shaolin Kung Fu Academy teaches the Chinese arts of Kung Fu, Tai Chi and other physical forms that generate constructive energy for harmony of the body, the spirit and the world. In these arts, the important point is that the mind is not separate from the body. How one moves and behaves in physical ways can affect and enhance how one can build a healthy lifestyle.

Pure Shaolin Kung Fu Academy was founded in 2005 with the primary focus on Northern Shaolin Kung Fu. Today, we have grown into a sports academy with many diverse goals such as promoting cultural exchange between China and the United States, providing the public with authentic instruction in Chinese martial arts, training the Wushu teams for national and international competitions and serving as a resource center for our students. Pure Shaolin Kung Fu has a variety of programs for all ages and skill levels from beginner to advanced. We strive to provide a positive learning experience for all of our students.

The word “Shifu” in Chinese is made of two words: “Shi” meaning teacher, and “Fu” meaning parent. As Shifus, we not only teach kung fu skills as a teacher, but also care about a student’s personal growth as a parent. Our goal is for our students to lead proper lives, interact respectfully with others, and work both independently and with a team. We want our students to learn to have a growth mindset, face challenges, and overcome them. These things take priority over the kung fu techniques we learn in class.

Breach the thresholds

Build Confidence and Self-Esteem

Activities to give students opportunities to make themselves proud

Belt testing requires a vast knowledge of your current belt level’s skills. In order to belt test, you must not only be deemed proficient in the Kung Fu skills you have learned, but also display growth outside of the studio.

Each time you take a belt test, you do three things: demonstrate that you understand and can execute the required skills to meet your new belt level, reaffirm your past knowledge, and display your recently acquired knowledge and skills. At the end of the test... celebrate! Celebrating builds your confidence and self-esteem. You deserve it!

Every year, our students go to many competitions on the local, national, and international level. Competitors are judged on a variety of criteria, including power, balance, and skill. While getting a medal is surely a rewarding event, competition isn’t just for getting medals. Competition provides a valuable learning experience unobtainable during regular class. The added pressure teaches students on how to handle stressful environments, while encouraging them to push themselves to constantly improve. In comparing themselves to their peers, students can gauge their current skill level while learning from the performances of their peers. There are no losers in competition. Every competitor learns something new with each competition.

Throughout the year, our students take part in many different performances. Performances allow students to increase their confidence and overcome stage fright, while also providing a platform for them to show off what they have learned. Performances also are a valuable community service, allowing the student to be an active member of their community.
